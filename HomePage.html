Indian cuisine is a blend of diverse cultural influences, spices, and cooking techniques.

Tandoori cuisine, originating from the Punjab region, is a hallmark style characterized by marinated meats cooked in a clay oven called a tandoor.

The meats are marinated in a mix of yogurt and spices, resulting in tender, flavorful dishes like tandoori chicken and paneer tikka.

Tandoori cuisine has gained popularity worldwide for its bold flavors and healthy cooking methods, becoming a staple in Indian restaurants globally.
